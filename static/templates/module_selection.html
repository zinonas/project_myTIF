Module 1: Patient consent, demographics and diagnosis

This module consists of 4 parts:
  1. Patient consent (obligatory)
  2. The minimum common dataset (obligatory)
  3. The sub-menu: proof of diagnosis (not obligatory)
  4. Family history and pedigree chart (not obligatory)

Module 2: Clinical Findings of Thalassaemia and Severe Anaemias (not obligatory)

In this module annual clinical data summary data are recorded, from patients who are regularly or irregularly transfused, whatever the diagnosis. The information can be used to follow progress or to follow the response to treatment, for example during a clinical trial.

Module 3: Clinical findings of Sickle Cell Disease (not obligatory)

In this module annual clinical summary data are recorded, from patients suffering from sickle cell syndromes. They are used in a similar way as module 2.

Module 4: Patient reported outcomes

This module records patient outcomes, such as patient reported outcomes (quality of life) and mortality. Especially useful for clinical trials. The module is not obligatory but mortality data are.

This module records aggregated data.
